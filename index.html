About Me

Electrical Engineer and an early career researcher focused and driven towards Embedded Systems, Control Engineering, Robotics, autonomous system and artificial intellegence. Currently working as a Junior Research Fellow on high frequency ultrasound transducer, design, development, characterization and scientific computing for therapeutic applications. I have been a keen explorer of the core sciences and its real world applications, often not bounded by a syllabus. It is my life's goal to become a polymath, cultivating a lifelong learning lifestyle that combines broad curiosity, deep mastery in multiple fields, and the ability to synthesize these domains into unique insights.
